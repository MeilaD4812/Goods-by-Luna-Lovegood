Butterbeer Cork Necklace

The Butterbeer Cork Necklace is used to keep away Nargles. The part that goes aroung the neck is blue and the butterbeer cork is attached to that. In the U.S. version of the Harry Potter books the butterbeer cork is replaced with butterbeer caps.
