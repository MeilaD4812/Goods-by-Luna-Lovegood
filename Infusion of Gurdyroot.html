Infusion of Gurdyroot

The Infusion of Gurdyroot is like a tea. In Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, Xenophilius makes this for himself, Harry, Ron, and Hermione when they are at his house. Harry, Ron, and Hermione think that it tastes bad but they drink it, so as not to be rude.
