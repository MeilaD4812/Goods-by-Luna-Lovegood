Xenophilius' Deathly Hallows Necklace

Xenophilius' Deathly Hallows Necklace is a necklace bearing the deathly hallows symbol. He wore it to Bill Weasly and Fleur Delacour's wedding. Victor Krum thought that it was Grindelwald's sign and got in a fight with Xenophilius about it.
