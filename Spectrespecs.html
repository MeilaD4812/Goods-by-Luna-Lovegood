Spectrespecs

The Spectrespecs are used to see Wrackspurts. According to Luna, Wrackspurts are tiny, invisible creatures that float in through your ears and make your brain go fuzzy. In an edition of the Quibbler magazine, a magazine that Luna's dad, Xenophilius, runs, there was a free pair of spectrespecs inside.
